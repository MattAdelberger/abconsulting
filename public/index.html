Asbestos

Thinking of performing renovation/demolition activities in your house? Remember to check for asbestos. Asbestos is a naturally occurring mineral that once was lauded for its versatility, recognized for its heat resistance, tensile strength and insulating properties, and used for everything from fire-proof vests to home and commercial construction. It was woven into fabric, and mixed with cement. Commonly affected building materials are drywall, plaster, ceiling tiles, roof shingles, and floor tiles. Materials that contain more than 1% asbestos are considered to be a regulated material which will need to be properly abated by a licensed contractor. We are a licensed Illinois and Wisconsin Asbestos Building Inspector. We will come to your building and collect samples for suspect asbestos containing building materials. We will then submit the samples to an accredited laboratory for analysis. Once the analysis comes back, A&B will write a report with recommendations.

Indoor Air Quality

Do you want to know what maybe ailing yourself or your children? Then let us help. As part of our IAQ sampling, we will obtain information regarding your symptoms including any adverse health effects. A&B will perform an interior and exterior inspection of the house looking for areas of water intrusion and identifying the source. A&B will also collect air and tape samples if needed to identify the types of mold within the structure. In addition, to the mold sampling, A&B will look at temperature, humidity, carbon dioxide, and potentially volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Finally, A&B will write a report with recommendations.
